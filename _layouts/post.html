Zecheng Li (Justin)

I am a final year undergraduate student pursuing a BS in Mathematics-Economics at the Department of Economics and a BS in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Machine Learning at the Department of Cognitive Science, and a minor in Data Science at the Halicioglu Data Science Institute. I am currently interested in the fields of machine learning, analytics, and econometrics. I am a data-driven person who never makes assumptions unless proven by data.

I am a research assistant (starting in January 2022) under the supervision of Prof Michael Davidson in the Davidson's Lab. My research areas are the transition of Chinese state-owned coal companies to carbon neutrality and labor resettlement issues based on The Consequences of the Thermal Coal Phase-out in China.

The best way to learn is to teach. Due to my interest in machine learning, I am now a teaching assistant for a hardcore upper unsupervised machine learning class (Cogs 118b), helping students during my office hours and through Piazza.

I wish more people had a better chance of getting to know me through this site. Unfortunately, there are still many projects that I would like to include on the site due to ongoing processes or privacy issues.
